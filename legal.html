Functional Cookies

  • Form data retention

Marketing Cookies

These are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Targeted advertising cookies
  • Social media integration cookies
  • Remarketing pixels

Our Commitment to GDPR

WenBot is fully committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We respect your privacy rights and ensure transparent data processing practices.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and analytics
  • Contract: To provide our trading services
  • Legal Obligation: For regulatory compliance (KYC/AML)
  • Legitimate Interest: For fraud prevention and security

Your GDPR Rights

Right to Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including:

  • What data we collect
  • Why we process it
  • Who we share it with
  • How long we keep it

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances:

  • When data is no longer necessary
  • When you withdraw consent
  • When data has been unlawfully processed
  • For compliance with legal obligations

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request restriction of processing in specific situations.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can request your data in a machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

Data Protection Measures

  • Privacy by design and default
  • Regular data protection impact assessments
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures
  • Third-party vendor due diligence

International Transfers

When we transfer data outside the EU, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Appropriate safeguards and binding corporate rules
