Pricing Questions

How is the 50% calculated?

The 50% fee is calculated only on your net monthly profits. If you don't make money, you don't pay anything. All calculations are transparent and visible in your dashboard.

What if I have a losing month?

If you have a losing month, you pay nothing. Our fee only applies to profitable months, ensuring you never pay during periods of losses.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. The 50% performance fee is the only cost. No setup fees, no monthly subscriptions, no withdrawal fees, no hidden charges of any kind.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can stop using WenBot at any time. There are no contracts or commitments. Simply disconnect the bot from your trading account.

How do you track my profits?

Through secure API integration with your broker, we track only the trades executed by WenBot. All data is encrypted and your privacy is fully protected.

What's the minimum account size?

We recommend a minimum of $1,000 to ensure proper risk management and meaningful returns. However, the bot can work with smaller accounts too.
